chicken alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is made by searing seasoned chicken breasts, boiling fettuccine pasta, and simmering a rich sauce of butter, garlic, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. The cooked pasta and sliced chicken are then tossed together in the sauce.

Ingredients

  • fettuccine pasta
  • chicken breasts
  • heavy cream
  • parmesan cheese
  • unsalted butter
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • italian seasoning
  • nutmeg
  • fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Cook fettuccine in salted water, drain, and reserve some pasta water.
  2. Season chicken: Coat chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  3. Cook chicken: Sear chicken in a hot skillet until cooked through, then slice
  4. Sauté garlic: Melt butter in the same skillet and cook minced garlic until fragrant.
  5. Simmer cream: Pour in heavy cream and simmer gently for a few minutes.
  6. Thicken sauce: Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  7. Combine: Toss the cooked pasta and sliced chicken directly into the sauce.
